About the project

After going through various courses, this was one of my first self-directed projects. During alockdown, when I had excessive free time, I invested some of it in coming up with thisproject. While I was designing, the aim was to reduce the itchy feeling due to longcontact with the skin. I used Fusion 360 to model and render it.
